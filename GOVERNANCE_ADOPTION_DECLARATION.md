Purpose

This document declares that this repository adopts Prospera OS Governance as its governing framework.

It establishes that governance authority, rules, and enforcement logic are defined externally and are not authored, modified, or overridden within this repository.

Scope

This declaration applies to all engineering, documentation, and AI-assisted activities conducted within this repository.

It does not introduce governance rules, authority definitions, or enforcement mechanisms.

Adopted Governance Source

This repository adopts the following governance source as authoritative.

Prospera OS Governance
Repository: ai-governed-software-engineering
Version: v0.2

All governance specifications, authority definitions, enforcement rules, and phase boundaries are defined and maintained in the adopted governance source.

Authority Declaration

This repository does not define governance authority.

All authority interpretations, decisions, and escalation paths are governed by the adopted governance source.

Project maintainers may not redefine or override governance authority within this repository.

Change and Modification Policy

Governance documents are not authored or modified in this repository.

Any required change to governance rules must be proposed and approved in the adopted governance source repository.

Local modifications that conflict with the adopted governance are invalid.

Audit and Compliance

This repository is subject to governance audit and review under the adopted governance source.

All activities must remain traceable and compliant with the referenced governance specifications.

Authority and Precedence

This declaration is subordinate to the adopted governance source.

In case of conflict, ambiguity, or omission, the adopted governance source takes precedence.
